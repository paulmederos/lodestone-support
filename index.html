The stone that points you true

A lodestone is the naturally magnetized rock early navigators used as a compass: the thing that finds north. This one finds your note.

No menus. No setup.

The whole screen is the instrument.

Open Lodestone and it's already listening. Play a string and the screen floods with that note's color, a big letter tells you where you are, and a field of particles tightens from a loose cloud into a solid ring as you lock in. You're looking at that same engine right above; it's Lodestone running in your browser.

🌈

Color is the reading

Every note has its own hue: low pitches glow red, high pitches violet. One trip around the rainbow per octave.

🎯

Tuned to a hair

A hand-rolled YIN pitch engine, validated to 0.05 cents. Rock-steady even on a low E, where cheap tuners get jittery.

✨

You feel the lock

The particles settle, the orb fills, the edges glow, and you get a soft tap the instant you're in tune.

🎸

Anything with strings

It's chromatic, so guitar, ukulele, bass, violin, banjo, mandolin and every alternate tuning are all covered, no instrument to switch.

🔒

Nothing leaves the device

Audio is processed live to find the note and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

🍃

Made with care

Zero ads, zero accounts, zero tracking. A small, independent app built because the free tuners deserved better.
