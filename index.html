FAQ

What instruments does it work with?

It's chromatic, so it hears whatever you play — guitar (standard, drop, and alternate tunings), ukulele, bass, violin, cello, banjo, mandolin, and more.

Do I need to set anything up?

No. Open the app and it's already listening. Play a string and the screen shows you the note and how close you are to in tune.

Does it record or send my audio?

No. Lodestone uses your microphone only to detect the note you're playing. Nothing is recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

Privacy

Lodestone does not collect, store, or share any personal data. Microphone audio is processed live on your device to detect pitch and is never saved or transmitted.
