A beautiful guitar tuner

A compass for your strings

A lodestone is the naturally magnetized rock early navigators used as a compass: the thing that finds north. This one finds your note.

No menus. No setup.

The whole screen is the instrument.

Open Lodestone and it's already listening. Play a string and the screen floods with that note's color, a big letter tells you where you are, and a field of particles tightens from a loose cloud into a solid ring as you lock in. You're looking at that same engine right above; it's Lodestone running in your browser.

🌈

Color is the reading

Every note has its own hue: low pitches glow red, high pitches violet. One trip around the rainbow per octave.

🎯

Tuned to a hair

A hand-rolled YIN pitch engine, validated to 0.05 cents. Rock-steady even on a low E, where cheap tuners get jittery.

✨

You feel the lock

The particles settle, the orb fills, the edges glow, and you get a soft tap the instant you're in tune.

🎸

Anything with strings

It's chromatic, so guitar, ukulele, bass, violin, banjo, mandolin and every alternate tuning are all covered, no instrument to switch.

🔒

Nothing leaves the device

Audio is processed live to find the note and is never recorded, stored, or sent anywhere.

🍃

Made with care

Zero ads, zero accounts, zero tracking. A small, independent app built because the free tuners deserved better.

Why I made this

I came to music late. For most of my life I figured it just wasn't for me. Being "a music person" felt like something you either were or weren't, and I wasn't.

Then a couple of years ago, something flipped. I decided I'd become someone who plays. I started with violin, stuck with it for a year, and got to a place I was proud of. Then a stretch of piano. Now I'm almost two years into guitar, and I play every day.

Here's what I found: anybody can play music. You just need a little space, some good coaching, and a bit of perseverance. That's really it. Music has become one of my favorite things, and I think more people should get to feel it.

So I made the tuner I always wanted, the one I actually love picking up every day. I hope you love it too. 🙏

peace,
Paul

What instruments does it work with?

It's chromatic, so it hears whatever you play: guitar, ukulele, bass, violin, banjo, mandolin, and every alternate tuning. Nothing to pick.

Do I need to set anything up?

No. Open it and it's already listening. Play a string and the screen shows you the note and how close you are.

Does it record or send my audio?

No. Your mic is used only to detect the note, live on your device. Nothing is recorded or sent. Privacy policy.
